The Kernel Polynomial method

The Kernel Polynomial Method (KPM) is a technique that allows for expanding spectral functions in terms of polynomials. The advantage of this method is that many of the typical problems such as Gibb's oscillations, finite size effects, and cubic or quadratic scaling with system size, can be overcome by choosing carefully the regularization parameters.
